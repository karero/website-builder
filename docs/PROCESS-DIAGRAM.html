How website-builder gets you to a site that ranks well and gets found

This is the journey your AI assistant walks you through when it builds your site with website-builder — whether that's a small business, a non-profit, or a personal or fan site. Three things get built in along the way, not bolted on at the end:

SEO
Search Engine Optimization — showing up in Google and Bing results.
GEO
Generative Engine Optimization — showing up when people ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Gemini a question.
EEAT
Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust — the proof points that make people, and search engines, actually trust the site.

See GETTING-STARTED.md for the plain-language walkthrough, or the README for the full picture.
